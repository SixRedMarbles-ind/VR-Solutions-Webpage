About Us

Who We Are

At Six Red Marbles, we’ve spent decades pushing the boundaries of content and technology in education. Today, we’re expanding that mission—bringing immersive Virtual Reality experiences into not just learning, but into how people engage, explore, and evolve.

Our VR solutions are built to go beyond the classroom or the training floor. We design experiences that simulate environments, provoke emotion, spark behavior change, and make abstract concepts tangible. Whether it’s a medical training lab, a soft-skill roleplay, an interactive product showcase, or a virtual cultural journey—our goal is the same: create moments that matter.

We bring together proven learning science, human-centered design, and immersive tech to help organizations deliver VR with purpose and impact.

Why VR – Why We Got Into It

  • Because the world doesn’t just read or watch anymore—it experiences.
  • We saw a need for more experiential, memorable, and realistic ways to communicate knowledge, values, and skills.
  • Learning that sticks: VR offers active, first-person engagement—ideal for deep learning, practice, and retention.
  • Behavioral transformation: VR lets you simulate real-life pressure, feedback, and reflection.
  • Scalable experiences: Deploy training, demos, and protocols anywhere—no physical limits.
  • Emotional resonance: VR connects in ways static content can’t.
  • Strategic innovation: We help our clients lead the next frontier, not follow trends.

Our Developer Network

With deep experience working with international partners and clients, including government bodies, universities, and corporates, we bring a global perspective grounded in local excellence. Our extended teams in France and Romania strengthen our capacity and innovation—most notably, we are proud to have contributed to government-backed VR initiatives in France, developing solutions for public services and education.
