Gamified VR for Safer Workplaces

At Six Red Marbles, we merge immersive VR with game-based learning to transform industrial safety training. By turning compliance and hazard awareness into interactive experiences, learners can build skills faster, safer, and with greater confidence.

  • Simulates critical workflows with stepwise guidance
  • Card Memory Game – strengthens recall, concept pairing, and attention to detail
  • CCTV Simulation – develops vigilance by spotting hazards in real-time feeds
  • ATEX Label Reconstruction – makes compliance training hands-on through 3D interaction
  • 3D Drag & Drop Hazard Identification – reinforces safety awareness in spatial, intuitive ways

Advantages

  • Safer practice with zero real-world risk
  • Game mechanics drive engagement and motivation
  • Standardized compliance and SOP training
  • Spatial learning improves recall and retention
  • Scalable modules across industries and teams
  • Lower cost than repeated physical workshops
